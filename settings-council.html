Tenth Man

During escalation, the single highest-ranked model is held in reserve and never votes with the pool. It then critically reviews the pool's consensus — challenging assumptions, testing the strongest dissent against the criteria — and casts the final, binding decision. This prevents the best model from anchoring the crowd.
